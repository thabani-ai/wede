About GreenSteps Foundation

Our Story

GreenSteps Foundation was founded in 2021 in Cape Town to mobilise communities in the fight against climate change. We focus on tree-planting, beach and river clean-ups, and environmental education in schools.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Fighting climate change at a grassroots level while empowering communities to live in harmony with nature.

Vision: A greener South Africa where every community actively restores and protects local ecosystems.

Core Values

  • Sustainability
  • Community Empowerment
  • Education
  • Transparency
